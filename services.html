Services and Therapies

The centre offers a wide range of services for parents and staff including clinical assesments, home plans for the childer and parents, staff training programs and different workshops.

Speech & Language Therapy: 

This aims to identify communication issues within children on the autism spectrum. Speech and language skills can be targeted with various therapy techniques e.g. play skills, video modelling and presentations.

Group Sessions:

These sessions allow the children to interact and communicate with each other with the presence of speech therapists and psychologists. This helps them learn the skills they would need for different interactions. 

ABA Therapy:

Applied Behaviour Analysis is a type of therapy that focuses on improving specific behaviours such as social skills, communication, reading and academics, as well as adaptive learning skills.
