President Message

Autism is a very poorly understood disorder, however its incidence is rising globally. Dealing with a disorder like autism is a lifetime commitment for the family. As a family effected by autism I established this centre with the aim to provide a platform of eduction for other effected families. The centre also works towards educating society for a better understanding and acceptability of autism. We do not want to isolate these chuldren but help them be self sufficient in their lives and integrate into society. The cost of such a project is enormous, the centre is run on a cost recovery basis to reduce financial burden on parents. I hope other members of society will help us achieve this mission. 
With Best Wishes,
Dr.Ahmed Faraz Bhatti
